Got Questions? Look Here

Do you have a question about the service of Appilix? See the list below for our most frequently asked questions. If your question is not listed here, then please contact us.

Do I need a Google Play or App Store Developer Account?

Yes you need to have your own Google Play or App Store Developer Account if you want to publish the app in Google Play Store or App Store.

What kind of websites can be converted into App?

Any website, built using any language or service can be converted into an app using Appilix. You don't need any additional coding or reconfiguration in your website.

Will Appilix slow down my website?

  • Absolutely not. Appilix carefully build you app with performance in mind. App users are like your regular website visitors.

  • You don't need to install or configure anything in your website to convert it into app.

Is there a trial period or a free version available?

Yes you can test using our Free Subscription plan to understand how Appilix can convert your website into an App. The free plan does not even need any payment details.

Are there any discounts available for bulk purchases or for non-profits?

Yes, you contact us at [email protected] to let us know your usage information and we may provide you discount if eligible.

Do I need coding skills to build the App?

Appilix can convert your website into an app without any coding by you. It's that simple like give us your website address and have your app ready.

What will happen if I update my site content later?

Appilix loads your website as an app so if you update your website content or design, your app will start showing the updated content automatically.

How many apps I can have in one purchase?

  • Each subscription plan (yearly or lifetime) is for one app. But you can customize the app and build again and again.

  • i.e. if you want 2 apps for your 2 websites, you will need 2 plan purchased.

Is there a money-back guarantee if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Yes, definitely. You can let us know via our Contact Page or Support email within 30 days of your purchase and you will get full refunds without questioning.

Are there any additional fees or hidden costs I should be aware of?

There are no additional fees required after your purchase. Also you don't have to pay anything extra for the support.
